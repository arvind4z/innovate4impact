About the Event

What is Innovate 4 Impact 2026?

Innovate 4 Impact: AI4SDG Global Hackathon 2026 is an elite international-level social impact innovation challenge organized by the IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) Affinity Group (SBA31281), in association with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Bharati Vidyapeeth (Deemed to be University) College of Engineering, Pune.

The event encourages undergraduate students to develop innovative, scalable, and technology-driven STEM solutions that address real-world societal challenges, with a focus on AI for the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), healthcare, women's safety, smart technology, and eco-friendly development.

Inclusion Mandate

Open to all UG students across India and outside India. At least one female team member is mandatory inside each group of 2-4 members — because real innovation is inclusive.

Evaluation Journey

The 3-Round Hybrid Challenge

A structured multi-round evaluation designed to test your innovation, technical skill, and real-world social impact potential.

01
Online Screening

Idea screening

Submit your abstract and a structured PPT (7–9 slides) covering: Problem Statement, Proposed Solution, Innovation, Technical Approach, Social Impact, and Feasibility.

Abstract Submission:Mandatory
Presentation:7–9 Slides
Format:PPT / PDF / Word
Initial Filter Phase
02
Online Evaluation

Technical Progress Evaluation

Submit your structured Progress Report (PDF) alongside a recorded progress showcase video (5-7 minutes). Demonstrate prototype milestones, active codebase progress, and expected deployment scope.

Deliverable 1:Progress Report (PDF)
Deliverable 2:Video Presentation
Duration:5–7 Minutes
Medium:English or Hindi
Progress Tracking
03
Offline Showcase

Grand Finale

A high-adrenaline 2-day offline finale hosted directly at BV(DU)COE, Pune. Present your absolute functional prototype, MVP, or demonstrable PoC where absolute product novelty is highly rewarded by the jury.

Venue:BV(DU)COE Pune Campus
Pillars:Functional Prototype / MVP
Presentation:8–10 Min Demo
Q&A:5 Min Judge Q&A
24-Hour Final Sprint

Judging Metrics

How You'll Be Evaluated

Review our transparent scoring frameworks deployed across all vetting panels.

30%

Innovation & Creativity

Evaluation of the absolute conceptual novelty and dynamic thinking model behind your resolution path.

30%

Social Impact

Relevance to chosen United Nations SDG goals and overall benefit to vulnerable user groups.

25%

Technical Implementation

Robustness of code infrastructure, framework stability, layout, security, and API response.

10%

Presentation & Demo

Confidence of pitch delivery, slide structure clarity, and smooth handling of live Q&A reviews.

5%

Industry Applicability

Feasibility of commercializing the concept and ease of integration into active market avenues.
